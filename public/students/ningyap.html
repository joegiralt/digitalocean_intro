Student Profile * Summer Semester 2013

Ning Yap

Ready for an Adventure in Ruby Coding

It's Day One of Flatiron School, and I'm thinking what the @#%&! did I get myself into? I just finished my last code challenge yesterday at 2 am, totalling about 100+ hours of pre-work in 6 different computing languages. I feel like after conquering that mountain of information, my brain must have retained at least 30% of what I read. But here I am, staring at a browser window, my 3 courageous teammates that I've been grouped with, each scratching our brains wondering "how are we going to create our profile page?" I just can't remember anything.

"I've made a huge mistake"
                            -Gob Bluth, Arrested Development

Before Flatiron

I've been working for 4 years in various ad agencies in an IT support capacity. Computer knowledge and fluency is something I've picked up on the job, so learning on the fly is what I'm used to. Prior to that, I've worked in television as a Production Assistant on shows such as VH1's "I Want to Work for Diddy". I have a degree in Music Business from USC.
