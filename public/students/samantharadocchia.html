Coder Cred/Projects

Stunable

Stunable is a clothing aggregator, recommendation-powered discovery engine, and visual commerce platform that provides users with an interactive platform for browsing clothing online. Users enter a brand, product, mood, or occasion and Stunable responds by displaying clothes and accessories that are similar in style and taste. All vendor inventories are updated and maintained via the "Stunable Sync" API, which enables users to buy all products seen on the application.


Machine Elf Consulting

The Machine Elf is new breed of business advisement and consultancy services catering specifically to startup businesses in the tech industry. The company's interdisciplinary approach synthesizes traditional managerial, financial, and legal guidance with innovative and unconventional strategies, addressing the needs of the first-time entrepreneur who not merely wants to succeed but absolutely MUST. Additionally, Machine Elf develops its own proprietary applications for the gaming/augmented reality sectors.
