About Me

Once upon a time there was an entity called Print. This little girl was really good at navigating its digital workflow with her mini-spaceship. She learned to troubleshoot problems for the floating printing press in the sky, and this mini-captain became invincible and master of her own ship. But one day [queue Jaws theme] the internet aliens invaded the print universe and blew the printing press out of the ether. Luckily for the girl, she slept in late and missed the giant implosion. The end... sort of.

Aspirations

I'm an operations management consultant and a startup entrepreneur. I love working with designers and technical experts, and have been looking for a way to pivot out of print and into the tech space. I have a natural affinity for solving problems and can't help but weed out inefficiencies. Having co-launched a startup Googaro, my interests have grown to include product development. I want to learn to speak developer-ese so that I can more effectively communicate with hired staff, and to be able to make quick changes to the site as needed. Besides that, I can't wait to develop a product and take it from idea to implementation.
