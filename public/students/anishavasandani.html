Anisha Vasandani @ Flatiron School

Summer Semester 2013

About Me

Anisha was born in New Delhi, India and moved to the U.S. at the young age of 3. She's lived in Los Angeles for most of her life, growing up on Bollywood movies and loving Bar Method workouts. In May 2013, Anisha moved to New York to attend Flatiron School and make her mark in Silicon Alley!

Interests

  • volunteering and fundraising for Network for Teaching Entrepreneurship (NFTE)
  • exploring a city's gastronomic landscape and tweeting about it here @foodismynish
  • attending concerts and music festivals all over the country

Career and Aspirations

Anisha's former life consisted of tax accounting for media and entertainment companies in Los Angeles. In her spare time, she's dabbled in HTML and CSS when working on her blog.) At a point where tax became too taxing, Anisha decided to scratch that entrepreneurial itch. Instead of seeking out a CTO, she decided to take coding into her own hands! She's currently learning Ruby on Rails and aspires to develop web applications for her future e-commerce/food startup.
